# Chess Moves Template

> "Chess moves" are deliberate, multi-step plans that compound toward your 1-year, 3-year, and 10-year goals from `GOALS.md`. They're slower-moving than projects — the strategic layer between long-term vision and short-term execution.
>
> Use this folder for: career trajectory plans, big financial moves (real estate, equity decisions, asset acquisitions), relationship investments, skill-stacking sequences, location decisions, etc.
>
> Each chess move is its own .md file. Copy this template, rename it, fill it in.
