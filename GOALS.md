# Goals

> Your goals at multiple time horizons. Claude reads this every session and grounds advice in what you're actually working toward. Keep it living — update as goals shift.

## How to use this file
- **Be specific.** "Get healthy" is useless. "Run a 5K under 25:00 by August" is gold.
- **Make goals measurable.** A number, a date, a binary outcome.
- **Add gating conditions** — what has to be true before you can/should pursue this?
- **Update quarterly.** Mark progress. Move completed goals to ARCHIVE section.
- **Honest > aspirational.** Don't list goals you don't actually work on. Claude will reference these — fictional goals will produce fictional advice.

## Gating conditions (what has to be true before X)

Some goals shouldn't be pursued until certain conditions are met. List them here so Claude can flag when you're chasing a goal whose conditions aren't satisfied.

EXAMPLES:
- **[Big purchase, e.g. expensive car]** — gate on monthly income / expenses ratio of 5x. Until then it's a distraction.
- **[Quit day job]** — gate on 6 months of expenses saved + 50% of income from side work for 3 months running.
- **[Move cities]** — gate on remote work secured AND 3-month exploratory visit completed.
