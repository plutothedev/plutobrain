## Skill ideas worth exploring

- `/brain-export` — export the vault into a portable digest (markdown bundle + condensed CLAUDE.md) so a future you on a new machine can re-bootstrap in 5 minutes.
- `/contradict` — explicitly hunt for contradictions across notes (more aggressive cousin to `/lint`'s contradiction detection).
- `/decision-log` — structured record of decisions made, with the predicted outcome at the time. Useful retro material 6 months later.
- `/sunday-letter` — generate a "letter to future me" from the past week's daily notes + weekly review. Files to `04 Reviews/`.
